How to contribuite:

On Desktop:

Use the Keyboard shotcuts or click the buttons. Left Arrow Key to vote NO,   Right Arrow Key to vote yes,   and key to Undo last vote.

On SmartPhones :

Landscape Mode Recommended. Swipe the picture to the Left ( ← ) to vote NO,   Swipe the picture to the Right (→) to vote yes. Or click the buttons.

Vote on the first picture to continue, and this hint will disapear.
